The Malnad store

Welcome to Malnad Store, where we bring the essence of the Malnad region to your doorstep. Our online grocery store is dedicated to offering you the freshest and highest-quality products, straight from the lush landscapes of Malnad. Here's what makes us unique:

Authentic Malnad Products: Experience the rich flavors and unique ingredients that Malnad is known for. From traditional spices and masalas to fresh produce and homemade snacks, we have it all.

Fresh and Organic: We prioritize your health and well-being by providing fresh, organic, and locally sourced products. Our partnerships with local farmers ensure that you receive the best quality groceries, free from harmful chemicals and pesticides.

Wide Variety: Whether you are looking for everyday essentials or specialty items, our extensive catalog has something for everyone. From grains and pulses to dairy and bakery products, Malnad Store is your one-stop shop for all your grocery needs.

Affordable Prices: We believe in providing high-quality products at competitive prices. Take advantage of our regular discounts and special offers to get the best value for your money.

Sustainable Practices: We are committed to sustainability and eco-friendly practices. Our packaging is designed to minimize waste, and we encourage our customers to join us in our efforts to protect the environment.
